## Short Description

Prayer times, announcements, events, and Qibla tools for Darul Ummah Shadwell.

## Full Description

Darul Ummah Shadwell helps the local community stay connected with mosque prayer times, Jummah details, announcements, classes, events, notifications, contact actions, directions, and a Qibla compass.

The app includes live timetable information, reminders, announcement updates, mosque contact details, live video access, and secure donations through SumUp.

## Data Safety Notes

Use these notes to complete the Play Console Data safety form. Verify against the final app before submission.

- Location: used on device for Qibla direction. The app requests approximate/fine location.
- Photos/media: admin-only announcement image selection and upload can use device media.
- Notifications: local prayer/event reminders.
- Contact actions: phone, email, and maps are opened through external apps.
- Payments: donations are handled by SumUp through a hosted HTTPS payment page. The app does not collect or store card details.
- Ads: none.

## Review Notes

The Donate button opens a SumUp-hosted HTTPS payment page for mosque donations. Card details are handled by SumUp and are not collected or stored by the app.
